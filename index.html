Hi, I’m Zhijie Liu. I’m now a first-year Ph.D. student studying at The Ohio State University, under the supervision of Prof. Zhiqiang Lin. Previously, I received my Master Degree from ShanghaiTech University, where I was advised by Prof. Yutian Tang, Prof. Liangfeng Zhang (and Prof. Yuqi Chen). During my Master’s studies, I also interned at Tencent Keen Lab, supervised under Researcher Sen Nie and Qiyi Tang, working on binary analysis and threat intelligence researches. My current research interests include Software Security, Software Engineering, program and binary analysis, LLM Security, etc.
